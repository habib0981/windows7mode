Windows 11 Requirements

  • Processor: 1.6 GHz or faster, dual-core processor (or higher)
  • RAM: 4GB minimum, 8GB recommended for better performance
  • Storage: 20GB free space (32GB preferred)
  • Graphics: DirectX 12 compatible graphics card with WDDM 2.0 driver
  • Display: 720p resolution or higher
  • Internet: An internet connection is required to complete setup
  • Others: UEFI firmware, TPM 2.0 support

Optimized for Windows 11 users who want the nostalgic experience of Windows 7 without sacrificing modern security and features.
